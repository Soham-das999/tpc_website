Training

The Training Program aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations. It equips students with the essential technical skills, soft skills, and hands-on experience required to thrive in real-world professional environments. Through workshops, internships, certifications, and mentorship by industry experts, we ensure that students are not only job-ready but also confident and competent to take on future challenges. The program emphasizes continuous learning, practical exposure, and holistic development to prepare every student for a successful career path.

Placements

Our Placement Cell is dedicated to providing students with excellent career opportunities across various industries. We collaborate with leading companies to organize campus recruitment drives, offer interview preparation sessions, and conduct aptitude and soft skills training. With a strong network of recruiters and a focus on student readiness, we aim to ensure that each graduate steps confidently into the professional world. Our consistent placement records reflect our commitment to helping students achieve their career goals.
