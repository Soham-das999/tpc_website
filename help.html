Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the Training and Placement Cell (TPC)?

The TPC coordinates training sessions, internships, and placements to support student career growth.

2. Who can contact the TPC?

All registered students, faculty, and recruiters can contact TPC for placement-related queries.

3. How do I register for placement activities?

Fill the placement registration form available on the TPC portal and agree to the policies.

4. Is registration mandatory?

Yes, it's required to participate in campus recruitment drives.

5. Can I edit my registration details later?

Yes, within a limited window. After the deadline, you need TPC permission.

6. What are the eligibility criteria for placements?

Typically 60% minimum academic score with no active backlogs. Criteria vary by company.

7. Are students with backlogs eligible?

Generally no, but some training programs may allow it.

8. Can I apply to multiple companies?

Yes, but some policies may limit final accepted offers (e.g., one dream offer rule).
